FUBU – Daymond Johnson’s Clothing Brand

In his early twenties, Daymond Johnson embarked on a groundbreaking venture that would change the landscape of hip-hop fashion forever. It was in 1992 that Johnson founded FUBU, a clothing brand that revolutionized the industry with its bold and authentic urban aesthetic. Inspired by the desire to create a brand “For Us By Us,” FUBU quickly gained popularity among young, male customers who resonated with its message of inclusivity and cultural pride.

At the heart of FUBU’s success were its screen-printed t-shirts, which became iconic symbols of the hip-hop movement. The brand’s unique designs and high-quality products caught the attention of prominent figures in the music industry, leading to product placements in over 30 music videos. This strategic marketing approach propelled FUBU to unprecedented levels of success, generating a remarkable $350 million in revenue.
