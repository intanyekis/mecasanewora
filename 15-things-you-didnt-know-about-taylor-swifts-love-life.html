Taylor Swift hates articles like these. She doesn't want to be a sex symbol, she does not want people talking about her dating life, or with whom she might be doing the deed. She just wants to be left alone so she can sing and sell records, both of which she does extremely well.

But here's the thing- people want to know. Taylor has given herself a totally squeaky clean image, and to be fair, maybe it is not really an image, maybe she actually is squeaky clean. But either way, the more that she seems like the typical good girl, the more her fans and the media want to know about who she is dating, and also with whom she is doing the deed. It's just how it goes, really, in our pop culture-obsessed world. Also, she tends to go for guys who are bad boys, at least a little bit, and that just makes the story a little bit more appealing for everyone else, too. Oh, and I forgot one more important thing... Taylor is hot. None of the other stuff would really matter if she was just a talented singer with a clean public image, but the fact of the matter is, Taylor has more than a bit of a smolder going on underneath it all.

So, sorry Taylor, we know you hate stuff like this, but we are going to do it anyway. We still love you, though- maybe you'll be flattered by our attention. We can hope so, at least. In any event, here are 15 things you didn't know about Taylor Swift's love life.

15. She Rebounds From A Breakup Fast

So we all know that our girl just had an awful breakup with Calvin Harris. In the past that might have put her in a really bad spot both personally and with the media. She would have spent a long time licking her wounds. But not anymore. She has been hanging with Tom Hiddleston and apparently things are moving along fast. According to an article in Eonline she and Hiddleston are already chatting up the whole kid thing. The article says: "This is the kind of a man she would want to marry," the source said. "She has said he would make a great dad. They have talked about what they want in the future and kids are something that they would both like down the line. They have very real and serious conversations about life." That sounds like a good plan Taylor- just make sure you invite us to the wedding.

14. She Doesn't Want To Be A Sex Symbol

Taylor once said in an interview with Access Hollywood that she did not see herself as a sex symbol. She said: "It's fine. I've accepted it. I'm a lot of things. Overtly sexy is not one of them. I'm fine. I'm fine with all the other things I am." She went on to say "As an artist you should embrace what comes naturally to you, whatever role feels right. I would never encourage an artist to try to fit in to a role that seemed to be created for them by a bunch of record label guys in suits in a conference room. I think for me it feels very natural to talk to teenagers and people my age… about feelings and what they're going through and their insecurities."  You don't quite get it though, Taylor. The fact that you are so sexy and don't think that you are is kind of exactly what makes you so sexy in the first place. Sorry to be the one to break that to you.

The moral of the story is that if you see Taylor, don't grab her butt, no matter how much you want to. Just don't do it! If you think she screws around, check this out that was originally posted in Radaronline. The documents state that her goal in filing a lawsuit “will demonstrate that Mueller alone was the perpetrator of the humiliating and wrongful conduct targeted against Ms. Swift, and will serve as an example to other women who may resist publicly reliving similar outrageous and humiliating acts. For this reason, any recovery obtained by Ms. Swift will be donated to charitable organizations dedicated to protecting women from similar acts of sexual assault and personal disregard.” All we can say to that is "You go girl."

She's flattered because she thinks Orlando is so sexy and, in normal circumstances, she would leap at the chance." Okay, one of her friends said that? Anyway, despite all of Mr. Bloom's overtures, nothing ever happened, even though there was a lot in the press about them.

11. She Felt Shamed Into Being Single

Notice that this is all put into past tense? The key word here is "felt" shamed into being single and not "feels" shamed into being single; we've already established that she has something going on right now with a certain Mr. Hiddleston. In the past, though, she felt that way and talked about the fact that everyone talked about her at length to the media. She said this: "It’s kind of a sad way I got there, though, being shamed into it. What else is it when you have two boyfriends in one year and everyone’s calling you boy crazy, making jokes about you at awards shows? That’s public humiliation. And I don’t think it’s fair." Well, let's be honest Taylor, none of it is fair. It isn't fair that people are always talking about you, but it also isn't fair that a lot of people live in crushing poverty. Sometimes it's hard to have it both ways.

This sort of falls in line with the previous part of the article but Taylor has been known to get irritated when her sex and dating life have been brought up in interviews. She is cool to talk about her music, her tours, or any other project she has going on, but if one asks her about her personal life she gets a bit uptight. I understand this, I really do, I mean who wants that kind of thing to happen who is not named Kardashian??? At the same time, maybe Taylor just has to understand a few things. She is one of the very biggest stars in the entire world and she is hot. People are going to want to know about her. If I were her I would just get married and start popping out kids 'cause that's pretty much the only way that people will stop asking.

See, just right now I was not picturing you naked. Then I read a quote from you about how much you don't want people to picture you naked, which of course makes me picture you naked. In fact, right now there are probably thousands of people picturing you naked, purely because you said something about how much you did not want people to picture you naked. This is not rocket science Taylor! Anyway, I am distracted all of a sudden for some reason, so I think I am going to move forward to the next item on the list. Once I finish picturing Taylor naked, that is.

Taylor went out with Harry Styles for a while before she got dumped by him. Supposedly one of the reasons was "all she wanted to do was kiss." Now one can take this one of two ways. The other way we can take it is that when he said, "all she wants do is kiss," he really meant that is all she wanted to do, and did not want to go any further.
